Resident

How it works

The Resident membership is very special: Nomad Nation is helping you as a solopreneur by employing you. That might sound paradoxically, but it's true. Only through hiring you it will be possible for you to easily apply for long term residency as a non-EU citizen. Also as an employee you'll benefit from much reduced bureaucrativ overhead for your business.

But rest assured: Nomad Nation will leave you alone as much as possible. You do your own business, you stay independent in your client relationships. It's just that the revenue you generate will be payed out to you as a salary.

As a Resident member you'll pay yourself the minimum required Bulgarian salary of some 400€/mo in total (including income tax and all social security contributions). This much revenue you'll have to make at least (plus the monthly membership fee; see below).

And whatever you want to take out of your business on top you can pay yourself as a bonus in addition to the minimum salary. (The bonus will of course also be taxed and you pay social security from it.) You can decide yourself each month what your bonus should be; no need to ask a manager, just because you're employed. You profit is yours to keep.
